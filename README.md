## Founder of StefDevs

I help turn unclear technical, sourcing, operational, and commercial problems into researched options, clean systems, coordinated execution, and finished work.

My foundation is backend engineering: TypeScript, AWS, integrations, automation, distributed teams, failure analysis, and systems that need to keep moving while requirements are still unclear.

StefDevs exists for work that does not fit neatly into one box. Sometimes that means code. Sometimes it means supplier research, workflow design, documentation, coordination, internal tooling, or making the right refusal before a bad path becomes expensive.

The method stays the same: clarify the problem, map the constraints, compare options, define the next action, and leave behind something easier to operate.

**Clear. Lawful. Useful. Operable.**
